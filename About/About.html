Meet Our Team

Chef John Thompson

Lead Chef and Co-founder

Hailing from the heart of the American South, Chef John Thompson is a master of Southern comfort food with a modern twist. Drawing inspiration from his grandmother's kitchen and local farm-to-table ingredients, Chef John crafts soulful dishes that celebrate the rich culinary heritage of the region. From crispy fried chicken to buttery biscuits, his down-home cooking will transport you to the cozy comforts of a Southern family gathering.

Chef Maria Smith

Head Pastry Chef

With roots in traditional Mexican cuisine, Chef Maria Smith brings a burst of flavor to every dish she creates. Her passion for fresh ingredients and authentic flavors shines through in her signature recipes, from sizzling fajitas to zesty salsa verde. Known for her warm hospitality and creative flair, Chef Maria invites you to experience the vibrant and diverse flavors of Mexico in your own kitchen.

Chef Mei Chen

Head Pastry Chef

Born and raised in Shanghai, Chef Mei Chen brings a taste of authentic Chinese cuisine to the global culinary stage. With a focus on simplicity and balance, Chef Mei's dishes showcase the delicate interplay of flavors and textures that define traditional Chinese cooking. From savory dumplings to aromatic stir-fries, her recipes offer a glimpse into the rich culinary traditions of China, inspiring home cooks to explore the world of Asian cuisine with confidence.

Chef Luca Rossi

Head Pastry Chef

Trained in the art of Italian cooking from a young age, Chef Luca Rossi is a maestro of Mediterranean flavors and culinary craftsmanship. With a passion for seasonal ingredients and time-honored techniques, Chef Luca creates dishes that celebrate the rustic simplicity and robust flavors of Italian cuisine. From handmade pasta to slow-simmered sauces, his recipes invite you to savor the timeless traditions and rustic charm of the Italian countryside.
